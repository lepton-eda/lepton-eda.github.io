4.1 No Connection Short Form Data Sheet

General description

The component is a mono‐pole with unique properties.

Applications

  • Digital circuits
  • Analog circuits
  • Mixed signal circuits
  • Consumer products
  • Industry products

Features

  • ESD tolerant
  • Latchup free
  • Operates with zero power supplies
  • Simple connection

Electrical Characteristics

Detailed Description

The component really does nothing, except making it perfectly clear that when it is connected to pins on other components, they should not, or at least need not, be connected to anything else.
